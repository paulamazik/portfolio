This time our task was to create an interactive animation. Me and my classmate created an interactive Snow White story. We recorded the voice recordings we used in the story, worked on the characters and the background.

The coding part was an exercise for us that helped us improve our Javascript skills. It was also an opportunity for us to work with the storyboard again and we were introduced to the sequence diagram for the first time which you can also see on the website.
