02.01.07 Storyboard


While being in the same group formations, after the treatment, we started working on out storyboard for the video.

It simply showes us how we wanted each shoot to look like and also it describes the location, sound, duration, the size of a shoot,the angle, transition.

It was the first time creating a storyboard. We had a few attemps since we made it way too general at the beginning. It involved lots of changes, understanding our mistakes. However it was a really useful tool while filming the video, and we used the knowledge a few more times later during the 1st semester.
